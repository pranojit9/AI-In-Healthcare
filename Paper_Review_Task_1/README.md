# Comparative Analysis of AI-Based Healthcare Research Papers

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comparative analysis of selected research papers focusing on Artificial Intelligence (AI) applications in healthcare. The study reviews recent publications from journals, conferences, and academic theses to identify emerging technologies, research trends, healthcare applications, and key contributions. The comparative analysis provides a comprehensive overview of AI-driven innovations in modern healthcare systems.

---

## 📚 Research Scope

The comparative study covers multiple AI-related healthcare domains, including:

- Medical Imaging
- Clinical Decision Support
- Deep Learning
- Healthcare Management
- Digital Health
- Clinical NLP
- Healthcare Cybersecurity
- AI Ethics and Governance
- Public Health Analytics
- Intelligent Healthcare Systems

---

## 🔍 Analysis Workflow

### 1. Paper Selection
- Selected recent and high-quality research papers.
- Included journal articles, conference papers, dissertations, and theses.
- Focused on AI applications in healthcare.

### 2. Literature Analysis
- Reviewed each paper in detail.
- Identified the research area and application domain.
- Examined the proposed methods and major contributions.

### 3. Comparative Analysis
Each paper was compared based on:
- Research Area
- Application Domain
- Major Contribution
- Publication Source

### 4. Research Summary
- Identified current research trends.
- Highlighted strengths of existing approaches.
- Summarized future opportunities in AI-based healthcare research.
